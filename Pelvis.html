The Pelvis

  1. Home
  2. The Pelvis
format_list_bulleted About this section add remove

The pelvis is the lower portion of the trunk, located between the abdomen and the lower limbs.

The pelvis’s frame is made up of the bones of the pelvis, which connect the axial skeleton to the femurs, and therefore acts in weight bearing of the upper body. The floor of the pelvis is made up of the muscles of the pelvis, which support its contents and maintain urinary and faecal continence.
